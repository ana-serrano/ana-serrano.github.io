ABOUT

SHORT BIO

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Graphics and Imaging Lab, Universidad de Zaragoza (Spain). I obtained my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Diego Gutierrez and Prof. Belen Masia. During my PhD I was the recipient of an Adobe Research Fellowship (honorable mention) in 2017, and a NVIDIA Graduate Fellowship in 2018. My thesis has been awarded with one of the Eurographics 2020 PhD awards.

RESEARCH TOPICS

My research spans several areas of visual computing, in particular: computational imaging, material appearance perception and editing, and virtual reality, with a focus on applying perceptually-motivated solutions. I am passionate about creating and applying fundamental knowledge about the perceptual system to improve human experiences and develop tools to assist content creation.
